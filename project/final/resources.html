Resources

Here are some additional resources that you can turn to for help. Although we hope our platform helps you see that many other students go through the same difficulties, we do not believe that it replaces in-person human connection. If you need counseling, please reach out to one of these sources.

UCD Health & Counseling Services

Website: https://shcs.ucdavis.edu/services/individual-counseling

Phone: (530) 752-2349

Location: North Hall

Details: No charge and eligible for all registered students

Undergraduate Career Exploration

Website: https://shcs.ucdavis.edu/services/groups/UCEG

Phone: N/A

Location: 219 North Hall

Details: Cost of assessment is $45 and eligible for all registered undergraduate UC Davis Students

UCD Health-e-Messaging

Website: https://shcs.ucdavis.edu/hemG

Details: Service is available at no charge, offers various online options to connect and communicate for healthcare and mental health needs

Eating Disorder Services

Website: https://shcs.ucdavis.edu/eating-disorder-services

Location: North Hall

Details: By appointment only, eligible for all registered students with service available at no charge.

Community Advising Network (CAN)

Website: https://shcs.ucdavis.edu/services/can

Phone: N/A

Location: Varies

Details: CAN Counselors proivde students with consultation and personal counseling.

NAMI(National Alliance on Mental Illness)

Website: http://namiucdavis.wixsite.com/namiatucdavis

Email: namiucdavis@gmail.com

Details: A mental health organization dedicated to build better lives for Americans who are affected by mental illnesses.
