Story Board

Setting:

People involved will include UC Davis students. The environment is student based and the ask being accomplished is to allow for students to share their struggles and their stories so that they will not feel like they are the only ones who are going through their own struggles.

Sequence:

The person will learn about this browser through the Student Wellness Center. It will be a website where students can engage with the student community. The steps that are involved include students looking up on the Student Wellness Center. The task that is being illustrated is how the interface design will look when entering the website.

Satisfaction:

The idea of students being able to read anonymous stories will motivate students to use this website. This way it will motivate people to share their stories and feel they are not the only ones to go through this. This enables people to feel more belonging. The need that it fills is to allow for students go through. Students are not limited to only sharing, they also have the option to read other's stories to still feel the sense of belonging.
